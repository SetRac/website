e-commerce order company
========================

This module sets online (ecommerce) order's company according to purchased products.

When you sell online products using different companies, you need use the correct company that will invoice to customer, and the correct payment method (say paypal account) where to receive money as well.
For example, if you have an English and a German company and you sell online a product in 2 versions, English and German, when customer buys the German product, they will pay using the payment method associated to German company. Moreover, the sale order will be associated to German company, so that delivery and invoicing will be handled by German company.
